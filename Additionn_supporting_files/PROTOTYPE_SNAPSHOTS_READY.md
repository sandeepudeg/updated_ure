## What's Included

### 6 Annotated Prototype Snapshots:

1. **Main Chat Interface - Mandi Price Query**
   - Shows farmer asking about tomato prices in Nashik
   - Market Expert agent provides real-time prices from multiple mandis
   - Displays modal, min, and max prices from Nashik and Lasalgaon markets
   - Source: Agmarknet (Live data)
   - Annotations: "6 Specialized AI Agents", "Real-time Market Prices"

2. **Crop Disease Detection with Image Upload**
   - Farmer uploads photo of diseased tomato plant
   - AI analyzes image and identifies "Early Blight"
   - Provides organic treatment (Neem Oil ₹150-250) and chemical treatment (Mancozeb ₹180-300)
   - Includes prevention strategies and application instructions
   - Annotations: "Upload Crop Photo", "AI-Powered Diagnosis"

3. **Government Scheme Eligibility & Benefits**
   - Farmer inquires about PM-KISAN scheme
   - Policy Expert provides complete details:
     - Benefits (₹6,000/year in 3 installments)
     - Eligibility criteria (small & marginal farmers, up to 2 hectares)
     - Required documents (Aadhaar, Bank Account, Land records)
     - Step-by-step application process
     - Helpline numbers (155261 / 011-24300606)
   - Annotation: "Complete Scheme Details"

4. **Mobile Interface - Touch-Optimized Design**
   - Shows mobile-first design with:
     - Quick action buttons (Disease Check, Market Price, Weather, Schemes)
     - Chat interface with sample conversations
     - Bottom navigation (Home, Chat, Prices, Settings)
     - Large touch targets for rural users
   - Annotations: "Quick Action Buttons", "Bottom Navigation"

5. **Weather Forecasting & Irrigation Planning** ⭐ NEW
   - Farmer checks 7-day weather forecast for Nashik
   - Weather Expert provides:
     - Daily forecast with temperature and rain probability
     - Irrigation recommendations (when to irrigate, when to skip)
     - Best timing for irrigation (6-8 AM or 5-7 PM)
     - Alerts for heavy rain and pesticide spraying guidance
     - Sowing recommendations based on weather
   - Annotation: "7-Day Forecast + Irrigation Tips"

6. **Rural Tourism - Extra Income Opportunity** ⭐ NEW
   - Farmer explores rural tourism for additional income
   - Tourism Expert provides:
     - Income potential breakdown (Homestay ₹15,000-₹40,000/month, Farm Tours ₹500-₹1,000/visitor)
     - Services to offer (farm stays, bullock cart rides, cooking classes, organic farming workshops)
     - Getting started guide (registration, amenities, listing platforms)
     - Initial investment estimate (₹50,000-₹1,50,000)
     - Support contact (Agri Tourism Development Corp)
   - Annotation: "Income Diversification Guide"
