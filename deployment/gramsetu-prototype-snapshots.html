Tell me about PM-KISAN scheme. Am I eligible?
📋 Sarkar Sahayak (Policy Expert)
🏛️ PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi Scheme

💰 Benefits:
• ₹6,000 per year direct to bank
• Paid in 3 installments of ₹2,000
• Every 4 months

✅ Eligibility:
• Small & marginal farmers
• Land holding up to 2 hectares
• All farmer families eligible
• No income limit

📄 Required Documents:
• Aadhaar Card
• Bank Account (Aadhaar linked)
• Land ownership documents
• Mobile number

📝 How to Apply:
1. Visit pmkisan.gov.in
2. Click "Farmer Corner"
3. Select "New Farmer Registration"
4. Enter Aadhaar & details
5. Submit land records
